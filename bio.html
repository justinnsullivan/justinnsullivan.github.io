Justin's Biography

Justin Sullivan was born in Queens and moved to the small town of Warren, NJ. Throughout his adolescence he played football and lacrosse, and guitar, acted in musical theatre, and fooled around with computers. In the fall of 2013, Justin began attending Tufts University. He is pursuing a BS in Computer Science and a minor in English, focusing on poetry composition. Justin is a member of the Beelzebub's, Tufts oldest all male a cappella group. In his down time Justin likes to cook, jam with friends, and read.
